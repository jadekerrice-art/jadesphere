Work in 2075: Automation and Reskilling

The risk isn’t automation — it’s unequal transitions. This is how exclusion becomes permanent.

By Jade Adamson•Published 2026•5 min read

The shift

AI compresses many tasks. Work reorganizes around judgment, integration, and human trust.

Winners and losers

People with access to tools, training, and networks accelerate. Everyone else risks stagnation.

Reskilling that works

  • Short cycles tied to real jobs
  • Employer-backed pathways
  • Credential + portfolio proof

Closing

By 2075, the policy question is simple: do we fund transitions — or pay for instability?
