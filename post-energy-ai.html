The Energy Cost of Intelligence

Compute growth, data centers, and what “sustainable scaling” must include.

By Jade Adamson•Published 2026•5 min read

Why energy becomes the bottleneck

AI scaling is not just an algorithm story — it’s a power, cooling, and grid story.

Where pressure shows up

  • Electricity load + peak demand
  • Cooling and water intensity
  • Supply chain + hardware cycles

What “sustainable” has to mean

Efficiency gains, transparent reporting, and grid modernization — not marketing.

Closing

By 2075, intelligence will be abundant. Energy is what decides how widely it’s shared.
